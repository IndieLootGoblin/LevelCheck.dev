/// features

Built for indie game design.

Not a generic feedback tool. Every feature is shaped for level designers, solo devs, and Steam Next Fest survivors.

Session recordings

Watch every input, every pause, every confused mouse-wiggle.

Difficulty spike detection

Flag the moments where players die, stall, or back-track far more than expected.

Level flow analysis

See the actual path players take vs. the path you designed.

Tutorial clarity testing

Find which mechanic taught itself and which one didn't.

Puzzle comprehension

Time-to-solve, hint requests, and abandonment per puzzle.

Rage quit tracking

Pinpoint the exact moment a player ALT+F4'd.

Steam demo readiness

A pre-launch checklist focused on first-impression friction in your Steam demo.

Playtester notes

Written, voice, and timestamped — synced to your build.
